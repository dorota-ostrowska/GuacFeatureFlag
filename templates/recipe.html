Ingredients:

  • 🥑 Avocados: 3 large ripe
  • 🫒 Olive oil: 4 tbsp
  • 🍋 Lime or lemon juice: 6 tbsp
  • 🧄 Garlic: 2 medium cloves
  • 🧅 Red onion: 1/2 small
  • 🍅 Cherry tomatoes: 6
  • 🌿 Fresh cilantro: a large handful
  • 🧂 Salt: 1/2 tsp
  • ⚫ Black pepper: a generous pinch
